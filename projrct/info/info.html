Avocado

Market value


Rs. 1250/kg

if you sell in retail for just for just 200/kg profit will be 10-12lakh per acre Rs. 1250/kg.

Micro Climatic conditions

ideal temperature should range between 3-32 degrees and relative humidity above 10%. Avocados do not tolerate frost, high temperatures above 42 degree Celsius, low relative humidity under 10% and extreme winds.

Water Considerations

10,000 cubic meter of water per hectare (including precipitation). for water in dry states you can you pit ponds (Acre plastic pond construction, capacity = 1 Cr litre (approx)).

Soil Tests

Tests to be done –
- EC, Calcium, Exchangeable Sodium, Soil Texture.

Irrigation system

I recommend using Netafim’s Uniram. It is a pressure compensated drip irrigation system.
