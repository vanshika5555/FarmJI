VERMICOMPOSTING

Market value

- Average market value of vermicompost: ₹10-15 per kg (wholesale), ₹20-30 per kg (retail)
- Premium quality vermicompost: ₹50-100 per kg

Micro Climatic conditions

-Temperature: 25-30°C (ideal for worm activity)
- Humidity: 60-80% - pH: 6.5-7.5 (neutral to slightly alkaline)

Water Considerations

- Moisture content: 60-80%
- Watering frequency: once a week (depending on climate and moisture levels).

Soil Tests

Tests to be done –
- pH testing: every 3-6 months.
- Nutrient analysis (NPK): every 6-12 months.
- Microbial analysis (worm population): every 3-6 months

Irrigation system

- Drip irrigation or sprinkler systems for uniform moisture distribution.
- Watering schedule: based on soil moisture levels and climate conditions.
